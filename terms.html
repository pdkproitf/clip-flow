5. TikTok Integration & API Usage

ClipFlowApp integrates with TikTok's Open API platform via OAuth 2.0 + PKCE to publish video content on your behalf. By connecting your TikTok account you explicitly authorise ClipFlowApp to act on your behalf for the following limited purposes only:

5.1 TikTok API Scopes Requested

ScopeAPI EndpointPurpose
video.upload POST /v2/post/publish/video/init/ & chunked PUT upload Initialise a Direct Post on TikTok and upload the video bytes for a scheduled clip.
video.publish POST /v2/post/publish/status/fetch/ Poll publish status after upload and retrieve the public post ID once the video is live.
user.info.basic GET /v2/user/info/ (fields: open_id, display_name) Retrieve your TikTok display name and account identifier at connect-time to display in the ClipFlowApp interface.

5.2 What ClipFlowApp Does With TikTok Access

  • Uploads video clips only when you have explicitly scheduled them.
  • Polls the TikTok publish status endpoint to confirm a clip has gone live.
  • Reads your display name and open_id once at connect-time; this data is never shared.
  • Automatically refreshes your access token using the TikTok refresh token flow to ensure uninterrupted publishing.

5.3 What ClipFlowApp Does NOT Do With TikTok Access

  • Does not read your TikTok feed, follower list, analytics, messages, or liked videos.
  • Does not post to any TikTok account other than the one you personally connected.
  • Does not store TikTok video content beyond the time needed to upload it.
  • Does not sell, transfer, or share your TikTok credentials or data with any third party.
  • Does not use TikTok data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the publishing workflow described above.
